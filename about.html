About Us

Our Story

Next Ledgers was founded in 2024, but our Journey began over a decade ago. It is a team of highly qualified, talented, and experienced professionals who came together with one shared vision: to redefine what international accounting support should feel like for firms in the US and Canada.

Our founder, Durgesh Pandey , is a qualified ACCA professional with a Master’s degree in Accounting and Tax. With over 10 years of hands-on experience in accounting, auditing, and tax consulting across North America, Durgesh has built a solid reputation for expanding firms, especially small and growing CPA practices with the tools, processes, and support they need to scale.

Before starting Next Ledgers , Durgesh partnered with a leading outsourcing firm, where he played a crucial role in helping 10+ CPA firms across the US and Canada transform their operations. From automating workflows to assisting clients in acquiring entire CPA practices, his strategic guidance helped those firms move from surviving to thriving.

Why We Started Next Ledgers?

We didn’t build Next Ledgers to be “just another accounting service provider”. We started it because we saw a gap — too many firms were onboarded and then left unsupported.

At Next Ledgers, we believe in continuous care, not one-time connections. Our clients don’t just meet one person during onboarding; they work with a responsive, experienced team that’s invested in their growth and success at every stage.

Whether you're looking to streamline your accounting, expand your client base, or acquire a new practice, we bring not just service but strategy, experience, and partnership.
